A Stone Forest

Designed by Antoni Gaudí, this basilica is a fusion of Gothic tradition and organic Art Nouveau. The interior columns branches out like trees, creating a "stone forest" that filters light through massive stained-glass windows.

The eastern windows glow with cool morning blues, while the western windows ignite in fiery sunset oranges.

Hidden Details

  • Construction began in 1882 and is famously still ongoing, with completion estimated for 2026.
  • Gaudí used complex ruled surfaces like hyperboloids and paraboloids to create the organic shapes.
  • Once finished, the Tower of Jesus Christ will make it the tallest church building in the world.
  • On the Passion Facade, there is a 4x4 grid of numbers. No matter which way you add them—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally—the sum is always 33.
