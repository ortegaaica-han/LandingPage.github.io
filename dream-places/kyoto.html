Kyoto is the cultural heart of Japan. With over 2,000 temples and shrines, it offers a peaceful contrast to the neon lights of Tokyo. My dream is to walk through the thousand vermillion gates of Fushimi Inari and experience a traditional tea ceremony in Gion.

Zen Insights

  • Temple Wealth: Kyoto has 1,600 Buddhist temples and 400 Shinto shrines.
  • Imperial History: It served as Japan's capital and the Emperor's residence from 794 until 1868.
  • Geisha Culture: The Gion district is one of the few places left where you can still spot Geiko and Maiko.
