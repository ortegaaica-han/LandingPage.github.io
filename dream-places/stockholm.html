A City on the Water

Built on 14 islands and connected by 57 bridges, Stockholm is a masterclass in urban planning and sustainability. I'm drawn to its unique mix of medieval history in Gamla Stan and the cutting-edge, minimalist design that permeates the modern city.

Nordic Nuances

  • Nobel Prize: Stockholm is the home of the Nobel Prizes, which have been awarded here since 1901.
  • The Underground: Stockholm's subway system is often called "the world's longest art gallery."
  • The Vasa: The city houses the Vasa Museum, featuring a 17th-century ship that is almost 100% original.
